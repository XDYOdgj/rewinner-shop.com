Clear Acrylic Display Risers – Show Off Your Treasures with Style

These sleek, durable risers are designed to elevate your collection with a modern, transparent look that blends perfectly into any home or retail setting.

Built for Strength and Style

Manufactured from high-quality, crystal-clear acrylic, these risers are not only strong but also incredibly clear, allowing your items to shine without any obstruction. With a thickness of 3mm, they provide enhanced durability compared to standard plastic options, making them ideal for delicate collectibles like Funko Pops, Amiibo, and more.

Each set includes three different sizes, offering flexibility to display your favorites in the perfect arrangement, whether you have a small or large collection. The clean, minimalist design ensures that your display looks polished and professional.

These risers come in a rectangular shape that’s easy to stack, making them great for organizing and showcasing items like toys, plants, small decor, perfumes, and cosmetics.
