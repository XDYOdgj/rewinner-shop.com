Stylish Mezuzah Scroll Case with Elegant Floral Design

This mezuzah scroll case beautifully marries tradition with modern design, making it a perfect accessory for any home or a meaningful gift for special occasions. Its unique small flower motif, crafted from high-quality acrylic and aluminum, ensures both durability and aesthetic appeal.

Measuring 5 inches in height, the case is designed to accommodate a 4-inch mezuzah scroll with ease. It's not only functional but also enhances the overall look of doorframes and entryways. Whether you're decorating your living space or looking for a meaningful way to celebrate a wedding or a baby naming ceremony, this scroll case is the ideal choice.

The optional kosher scroll adds an authentic touch, offering flexibility to suit your personal or cultural needs. Thanks to its user-friendly peel and stick technology and included double-sided tape, installation is hassle-free and secure, making it a great feature for both home decor and gifting.

A smart addition to any interior, this mezuzah scroll case is both decorative and symbolic.
