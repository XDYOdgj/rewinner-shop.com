ELEGANTLIFE Acrylic Display Risers

Bring a touch of sophistication and organization to your home or retail displays with our sleek acrylic risers. Designed to showcase your favorite jewelry, crafts, and small decor items, these risers offer a layered aesthetic that enhances the visual appeal of any collection.

Each set includes 4 different sizes, allowing for creative stacking and arrangement. The premium transparent acrylic construction ensures clear visibility, letting your products shine in every detail. With a 4.3mm thickness, they are built to last and can support up to 800g of weight, making them perfect for display purposes rather than long-term storage.

Thanks to their pre-peeled corners, the protective film is easy to remove, giving you a clean start without extra effort. The multi-color option adds a stylish element to your setup, while the secure packaging guarantees that they arrive in excellent condition, ready to be used right away.

Whether you're arranging jewelry on a countertop or setting up a display in a boutique, these risers provide a modern and elegant solution for showcasing your treasures with confidence and style.
